Friends of my Ruslan and Lyudmila,reader, or where you've shone and glittered!Onegin, my good friend, was litteredwith this the hero of my tale:There once I too strolled back and forth:but I'm allergic to the North...where you were born as well, I think,and bred upon the Neva's brink,let me acquaint you on the nailwithout preliminary feelerthe legatee of his relations --Such were a young rake's meditations --as horses whirled him through the dust.by will of Zeus, the high and just,
